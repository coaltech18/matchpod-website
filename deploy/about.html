About Matchpod

We're on a mission to transform how people find roommates and build meaningful connections in Bangalore.

Our Story

Born from the real struggles of finding safe, compatible roommates in Bangalore

The Problem We Solved

As young professionals moving to Bangalore, we experienced firsthand the challenges of finding trustworthy roommates. The existing solutions were either unsafe, impersonal, or simply didn't work for the modern urban lifestyle.

We faced fake profiles, safety concerns, and the frustration of living with incompatible people. That's when we decided to build something better - a platform that prioritizes safety, compatibility, and community.

Our Vision

Creating meaningful connections through safe, verified roommate matching

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

Authentic Connections

Community Driven

Built by Bangaloreans, for Bangaloreans. We understand the local culture and needs of our community.
