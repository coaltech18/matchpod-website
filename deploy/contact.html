Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Matchpod

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process includes government ID verification, phone number verification, and social media integration. We also offer optional background checks for enhanced safety.

Is Matchpod available in other cities?

Currently, we're focused on Bangalore. We plan to expand to other major cities in India soon. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on new locations.

What if I have a safety concern?

Your safety is our top priority. If you encounter any issues, you can report them through our in-app safety feature or contact our 24/7 support team immediately.

How much does Matchpod cost?

Basic features are free. We offer premium features like Super Likes, profile boosts, and advanced filters through our subscription plans.
