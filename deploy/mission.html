Our Mission

Building meaningful connections through safe, verified roommate matching in Bangalore

Why We Started This Journey

We know what it feels like to move to a new city, full of dreams and aspirations, only to be met with the harsh reality of finding a safe place to call home. The stress, the uncertainty, the countless hours spent scrolling through listings with no guarantee of safety or compatibility.

That's why we created Matchpod - not just as another roommate finder, but as a social movement that reimagines what urban living can be. We believe that the right roommate can transform your experience of a city, turning a house into a home and strangers into lifelong friends.

Our journey began with a simple question: "What if finding a roommate could be as much about building connections as it is about finding a place to live?"

The Matchpod Promise

Our commitment to building a community based on trust, safety, and meaningful connections

"We promise to create a platform where every connection is built on a foundation of trust. Where safety isn't just a feature, but the core of everything we do. Where finding a roommate becomes the beginning of a friendship that lasts beyond the lease."
